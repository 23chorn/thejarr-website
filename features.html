Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Jarr does one thing well — helps you save with intention, inside the app you already use every day.

🎯

Goal-based saving

Create up to 3 named savings goals on the free plan — each with a target amount, optional deadline, and real-time progress tracking. Goals are yours to name: "House deposit", "Flight home", "Emergency fund".

Free plan: 3 goals
💬

Log deposits in seconds

Send an amount in any format and Jarr understands. "AED 500", "£200", "1000" — it's logged instantly in the right currency. No forms, no tapping through screens. If you have multiple goals, just prefix with a number: "2 AED 500".

Any currency
💱

Multi-currency support

Set one primary currency and up to two secondary currencies. Every progress update shows your total in all three. Rates are pulled from ExchangeRate-API — no markup, no guesswork.

You can log individual deposits in any currency regardless of your defaults. Jarr converts at read time so your history is always accurate.

USD · GBP · AED · INR · EUR
🤝

Save with a partner

Invite anyone on WhatsApp to share a goal — a spouse, flatmate, or friend. You both log deposits, see progress, and get milestone notifications. Each of you sees the total in your own primary currency.

Contribution breakdowns show exactly who has put in what — useful for split goals like a shared house deposit.

Free plan included
🏆

Milestone celebrations

Jarr cheers you on at 25%, 50%, 75%, and 100% of every goal. Small moments of recognition that keep you going when saving feels slow.

↩️

Undo a deposit

Made a mistake? Send "undo" within 24 hours to reverse your last deposit. Jarr soft-deletes it and notifies your partner if the goal is shared.

📁

Goal management

Archive goals you've paused, mark them complete when you hit your target, or delete them entirely. Archived and completed goals are preserved in your history — viewable any time with "history".

📱

Zero friction onboarding

No app store. No sign-up form. No email verification. Message Jarr on WhatsApp and you're set up in two minutes — name, currency, and first goal.

Works on any phone
