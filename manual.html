01

Getting started

Jarr lives entirely in WhatsApp. There is nothing to download, no account to create, and no email to verify. Your WhatsApp number is your identity.

First message

Tap the link below (or scan the QR code on the home page) to open a WhatsApp chat with Jarr. Send any message to begin — Jarr will guide you through a short setup in three steps.

Setup takes about 90 seconds. You'll be asked for your name, your primary currency, and optionally up to two secondary currencies to display alongside it.

Onboarding steps

  1. Name — Jarr asks what to call you. Enter your first name or any display name.
  2. Primary currency — Choose from AED, GBP, USD, INR, or EUR. All amounts are displayed in this currency by default.
  3. Secondary currencies (optional) — Up to 2 additional currencies shown alongside your primary in every progress update. Skip if you only need one.

After setup, Jarr confirms your preferences and prompts you to create your first goal.

02

Goals

A goal is a named savings target with an optional amount and deadline. You can have up to 3 active goals on the free plan, or unlimited on Pro.

Creating a goal

Send create goal to begin. Jarr will ask for:

  • Goal name — anything you like ("Dubai apartment", "Emergency fund", "Trip to Japan")
  • Target amount — in your primary currency. You can type the amount with or without the currency symbol.
  • Deadline (optional) — a date in any readable format ("Dec 2026", "31/12/2026", "next Christmas")

Start logging deposits any time.

Viewing goals

Send goals to see all active goals with a progress bar and percentage. If you have more than one goal, Jarr shows which one deposits will go to by default (marked with ← deposits go here).

Send status for a detailed view of your current goal — target, progress, remaining, and a progress bar.

Managing goals

  • archive — Soft-close a goal. It leaves your active list and frees up a free-plan slot. Deposit history is preserved and viewable in history.
  • complete — Mark a goal as done, at any progress level. Jarr celebrates with you and notifies your partner if the goal is shared.
  • delete — Permanently removes a goal and all its deposit history. Jarr asks for confirmation before proceeding. Your partner is notified before deletion if the goal is shared.
  • history — View all completed and archived goals with final progress. Shows the most recent 10.

Free plan tip: You can always free up a slot by sending archive on a goal you've paused. Archived goals keep their history — nothing is lost.

03

Logging deposits

Deposits are how you record money you've saved. Jarr accepts amounts in a natural format and logs them instantly.

Basic deposit

If you have one active goal, just send an amount:

You send: 500

Or: AED 500

Or: £200

Or: $300

Jarr logs the deposit and replies with updated progress, including all your secondary currencies.

Currency override

If you include a currency symbol or code, Jarr logs the deposit in that currency and converts it to your goal's base currency for the progress total. Your primary currency doesn't need to match the deposit currency.

Supported formats: £200, $300, €150, ₹5000, AED 500, 500 gbp

Multiple goals

If you have more than one active goal, Jarr deposits to your current default goal (shown in goals with a ← marker). To deposit to a specific goal without switching:

Goal number + amount:

2 AED 500 → logs to goal #2

1 £200 → logs to goal #1 in GBP

To change which goal receives deposits by default, send switch goal.

Undoing a deposit

Send undo within 24 hours to reverse your last deposit. Jarr will confirm the reversal and notify your partner if the goal is shared. Deposits older than 24 hours cannot be undone.

04

Currencies

Jarr supports five currencies at launch: AED, GBP, USD, INR, EUR.

Primary currency

Set during onboarding. All progress summaries and goal totals are shown in your primary currency first. Exchange rates are refreshed from the European Central Bank each time you check status or goals.

Secondary currencies

You can add up to 2 secondary currencies. These appear in parentheses after your primary in every progress update — for example: AED 18,500 (£3,975 · $5,030).

Changing your currencies

Send change currency at any time. Jarr will walk you through selecting a new primary and updating your secondaries. Existing deposits and goals are unaffected — only the display changes.

Exchange rates: Rates are mid-market rates from the European Central Bank. There is no markup. Rates are cached and refreshed when you actively check your progress.

05

Partner saving

Any active goal can be shared with one partner — a spouse, flatmate, friend, or family member. Both of you log deposits, see progress, and get milestone notifications.

Inviting a partner

Send invite partner. Jarr will ask which goal to share (if you have more than one) then send you a WhatsApp invite link to forward to your partner.

Your partner taps the link, messages Jarr, and is connected to your shared goal. If they're new to Jarr, they'll complete the 90-second onboarding first.

How shared goals work

  • Both partners see the total combined progress.
  • Each partner sees amounts in their own primary currency — you might see AED while your partner sees GBP.
  • Deposit confirmations show a breakdown of who has contributed what.
  • Milestone messages go to both partners simultaneously.

Partner notifications

Your partner is notified when:

  • You log a deposit to the shared goal
  • You undo a deposit
  • A milestone is reached
  • You archive, complete, or delete the shared goal
06

Summary image

Send summary to receive a PNG image in WhatsApp showing a table of all your active goals.

What's in the table

  • Goal — goal name
  • Primary currency total — progress in your primary currency
  • Secondary currency totals — one or two columns depending on your preferences
  • Last updated — relative time of the most recent deposit ("2h ago", "3d ago")
  • By — display name of the person who made the last deposit

A totals row at the bottom sums all goals. The image header shows your name and the generation timestamp.

Useful for: quick screenshots to share with a partner, reviewing all goals at once without scrolling chat history, or checking your overall savings position across currencies.

07

Settings

Change your name

Send change name. Jarr shows your current name, prompts for a new one, and confirms the update. Your name appears in partner notifications and the summary image header.

Change your currencies

Send change currency. You can update your primary currency, add or remove secondary currencies, or clear all secondaries. Existing deposit history is unaffected.

Subscription

Send upgrade to get a Stripe checkout link for the Pro plan ($4.99/month). To cancel, send cancel subscription — your access continues until the end of the billing period.
