01

Getting started

thejarr lives entirely in WhatsApp. There's nothing to download, no account to create. Message the bot and it walks you through a short setup — name, primary currency, and an optional secondary currency. The whole thing takes about 90 seconds.

Step 1 — Your name

thejarr →

Hi! 👋 Welcome to thejarr — your WhatsApp savings tracker.

Before we get started, what should I call you? (First name or nickname is fine)

You →

Sarah

thejarr →

Nice to meet you, Sarah! 🎉

Send your first name or a nickname (2–50 characters). This appears in messages, milestone celebrations, and partner notifications.

Step 2 — Primary currency

thejarr →

One more thing, Sarah — which currency do you primarily save in?

1️⃣ 🇺🇸 US Dollar (USD)

2️⃣ 🇬🇧 British Pound (GBP)

3️⃣ 🇦🇪 UAE Dirham (AED)

4️⃣ 🇮🇳 Indian Rupee (INR)

5️⃣ 🇪🇺 Euro (EUR)

Reply with a number or the currency code (e.g. '3' or 'AED').

Reply with a number (1–5) or a currency code such as AED or GBP.

Step 3 — Secondary currency (optional)

After setting your primary, thejarr offers one additional display currency. Pick one from the list, or tap Skip to continue with just your primary. The secondary amount appears in brackets alongside your primary in all progress messages — for example: AED 18,350.00 (£3,950.00).

thejarr →

All set! ✅

Your savings will be tracked in 🇦🇪 UAE Dirham (AED).

Ready to create your first goal? Send 'create goal', or 'help' to see all commands.

Setup only happens once. You won't be asked again unless you use change currency or change name.

02

Creating a goal

Trigger goal creation by sending any of: create goal, hi, hello, or start.

If your account has the WhatsApp Flow enabled, a native form pops up inside WhatsApp where you fill in the goal name, target amount, currency, and optional deadline — then tap submit.

If the Flow is not enabled, thejarr guides you through a text conversation:

You →

create goal

thejarr →

Let's set up your savings goal! What would you like to call it?

You →

Holiday Fund

thejarr →

Great! What's your savings target for Holiday Fund?

Send an amount with a symbol or code (e.g. '$5,000', '£2,000', 'AED 20,000').

You →

AED 20000

thejarr →

Got it — AED 20,000.00. Do you have a deadline in mind?

Send a date (e.g. 'December 2026') or reply skip to leave it open-ended.

You →

December 2026

thejarr →

✅ Goal created!

Holiday Fund

Target: AED 20,000.00 · Deadline: December 2026

Ready to log a deposit? Just send the amount (e.g. 'AED 500') anytime.

💡 Tip: Want to save with a partner? Type 'invite partner' anytime.

Note: A goal's currency is set at creation and cannot be changed. You can deposit in any other currency — thejarr converts automatically. Free plan users can have up to 3 active goals at once. Archive or complete a goal to free a slot.

Multiple active goals — no default set

thejarr →

Prefix any deposit with a goal number to skip the prompt and log directly to that goal:

This is also a one-time selection — it routes the deposit to goal 1 without changing your default. To make goal 1 your permanent default, use set goal.

Managing multiple goals

Use set goal (or switch goal) to choose which goal future deposits go to without making a deposit.

You →

set goal

thejarr →

[interactive list]

New Car — AED 500.00 / AED 50,000.00 (1%)

Emergency Fund — AED 0.00 / AED 10,000.00 (0%)

↩ Cancel

You →

thejarr →

✅ Got it — deposits will now go to Emergency Fund by default.

You can always use inline numbers (e.g. '2 $100') to log to a different goal without switching.

Important: set goal (or switch goal) is the only way to set a persistent default. Picking from the goal selection list when prompted, or using an inline number prefix (2 $100), routes that single deposit to the chosen goal but does not change your default.

If you only have one active goal, set goal simply confirms that goal is the target.

06

Undoing a deposit

You can undo your most recent deposit within 24 hours of logging it.

You →

undo

thejarr →

Your last deposit: AED 500.00 (logged 12 minutes ago)

Delete this deposit? Reply yes to confirm or no to cancel.

You →

yes

thejarr →

✅ Deposit deleted.

Holiday Fund

Updated progress: AED 4,500.00 / AED 20,000.00 (22.5%)

  • Only your most recent non-deleted deposit can be undone.
  • Deposits older than 24 hours cannot be undone — thejarr will explain this.
  • Partners are notified when a deposit is removed from a shared goal.
  • Reply no to cancel without making any changes.
07

Partner saving

Any active goal can be shared with one partner. Both of you can log deposits, see progress, and receive milestone notifications. Each partner always sees amounts in their own currency preferences.

Inviting a partner

You →

invite partner

thejarr →

Here's your invite link for Holiday Fund:

https://wa.me/YOUR_NUMBER?text=join-abc123xyz

Share this with your partner. The link expires in 7 days and can only be used once.

Once they accept, you'll both get updates on each other's deposits automatically.

If you have multiple goals, thejarr shows an interactive list to select which goal to share. Share the link via any WhatsApp chat — when your partner taps it on their phone, it opens WhatsApp and pre-fills the invite code.

After sending the invite, the bot asks whether you'd like deposit notifications from your partner:

thejarr →

Would you like to receive a WhatsApp notification when your partner logs a deposit?

[buttons: Yes, notify me · No thanks]

You can change this setting at any time with the notifications command.

Leaving a shared goal

Partners (non-creators) can leave a goal at any time using leave goal. The goal creator must use archive or delete instead.

You →

leave goal

thejarr →

Leave Holiday Fund?

Your deposit history will remain and count toward the goal. Alex will be notified and can re-add you later.

[buttons: Yes, leave · No, stay]

You →

Yes, leave

thejarr → (to you)

✅ You've left Holiday Fund.

Your contributions are preserved. If you'd like to rejoin later, ask Alex to send a new invite.

thejarr → (to creator)

Sarah has left Holiday Fund.

Their contributions are preserved. You can re-add them anytime with 'invite partner'.

  • All past deposits remain in the database and still count toward the goal's total.
  • The contribution split display only shows currently active partners, so departed partners no longer appear in the breakdown.
  • If the creator re-invites you and you accept, your history carries over and you appear in the split again.

09

Archiving a goal

Archiving soft-closes a goal. The goal becomes inactive (no more deposits), but all deposit history is preserved and visible in history. Archiving also frees up a slot if you're on the free plan.

Only the goal creator can archive. Partners are redirected to leave goal.

You →

archive

thejarr →

Archive Holiday Fund?

Progress: AED 5,000.00 / AED 20,000.00 (25.0%)

Archiving stops tracking but keeps your deposit history. This frees up a goal slot if you're on thejarr.

⚠️ Your partner will be notified.

[buttons: Yes, archive · No, cancel]

You →

Yes, archive

thejarr → (to you)

✅ Holiday Fund has been archived.

You can view it anytime with 'history'. Ready to create a new goal? Send 'create goal'.

thejarr → (to partner)

Holiday Fund has been archived by your partner.

The goal is no longer active, but your deposit history is preserved.

If you have multiple goals, thejarr shows an interactive list of your created goals. Tap a goal to select it, then confirm. Tap ↩ Cancel to exit without changes.

10

Deleting a goal

Deleting permanently removes a goal and all its deposit history. This cannot be undone.

Only the goal creator can delete. Partners are redirected to leave goal.

You →

delete

thejarr →

⚠️ Delete Holiday Fund permanently?

This will remove: the goal (AED 5,000.00 / AED 20,000.00), all deposit history, any partner connections.

⚠️ Your partner will be notified before the goal is deleted.

This cannot be undone.

[buttons: Yes, delete · No, cancel]

You →

Yes, delete

thejarr → (to partner, sent first)

⚠️ Holiday Fund has been deleted by your partner.

The goal and all deposit history have been permanently removed.

thejarr → (to you)

✅ Holiday Fund and all its history have been permanently deleted.

Partner notification is sent first. thejarr notifies any partner before deleting the goal, so they're aware before the data is gone.

If you have multiple goals, thejarr shows an interactive list of all your goals — including completed and archived ones, tagged [completed] or [archived]. Tap a goal to select it, then confirm.

Weekly savings digest (thejarr+ only)

  • Opt out at any time by sending notifications and toggling option 2️⃣.
  • The digest image uses the same format as the on-demand summary command.

Cancelling an action

At any point during a multi-step flow — goal creation, archive confirmation, invite response, etc. — send exit or cancel to abandon the current action and return to idle.

You →

exit

thejarr →

No problem, I've cancelled that. Send 'help' to see what you can do.

If nothing is currently in progress:

thejarr →

Nothing in progress to cancel. Send 'help' to see available commands.

The Cancel row in goal lists

All interactive goal selection lists include a ↩ Cancel row at the bottom. Tapping it exits without selecting a goal or making any changes.

Exchange rates

Rates are sourced from ExchangeRate-API and cached automatically. They are refreshed when you run status or goals. All historical deposits are converted at the current cached rate — not the rate at the time of deposit. There is no markup applied.

Dashboard overview

  • Victory Hall — lifetime savings totals: amount saved all-time, goals completed, and total deposits made
  • Top Partner — highlights the person you've saved the most with, showing combined savings and live progress on every shared goal
  • Goal cards — all active and completed goals sorted by deadline, each with a progress bar, amount saved, and percentage reached

Goal detail page

Tap any goal card to see the full detail view:

  • Progress card — currency breakdown table showing saved, target, and remaining in your primary and secondary currency
  • Burn-down chart — a line chart of how your remaining balance has decreased over time, with a dashed ideal line from start to deadline, a "Today" marker, and a pace badge (🔥 Crushing it / ✓ On track / Slightly behind / ⚠ Behind pace)
  • Monthly needed — exactly how much needs to be deposited per month to hit the target by the deadline, in all your currencies
  • Contributions — a stacked split bar showing each person's share visually, plus a ranked leaderboard (🥇🥈🥉) with name, amount, and percentage
  • Deposits list — every deposit in chronological order with depositor name, timestamp, and amount

Session security

  • Each thelid link is unique, short, and tied to your PIN
  • Sending lid or pulse again immediately invalidates the previous link
  • A Hold to Shatter Session button on the dashboard lets you manually expire the link at any time — useful if you shared a device or sent the link somewhere by mistake

thejarr+ feature. thelid is available on the thejarr+ plan. Send upgrade in WhatsApp to start the upgrade flow, or subscription / my plan to check your current plan.
