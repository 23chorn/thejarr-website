Questions

Frequently asked.

Do I need a credit card to start?

No. thejarr requires nothing but your WhatsApp number. You only enter payment details if you choose to upgrade to Jarr Together.

What happens if I hit 3 goals on jarr.?

thejarr will let you know and offer two options: upgrade to Jarr Together for unlimited goals, or archive or complete an existing goal to free up a slot. Your history is always preserved — archiving just removes a goal from your active list.

What is The Pulse?

The Pulse is a live web dashboard for Jarr Together members. Send pulse in WhatsApp to receive a secure link — valid for one hour — showing your full savings picture: burn-down charts, currency breakdowns, contribution leaderboards, and partner stats. Send pulse again any time to get a fresh link (the old one is invalidated immediately).

Can I cancel Jarr Together at any time?

Yes. Send "cancel subscription" in WhatsApp and thejarr will guide you through it. You keep Jarr Together access until the end of the billing period. After that, your account reverts to jarr. and your 3 most recent goals stay active.

Is my partner's WhatsApp number stored?

We store your WhatsApp number (required to receive messages) and your display name. We never store chat message content beyond what's needed to process your commands. Full details in our privacy policy.

Does my partner need a thejarr account?

Yes — when you invite a partner, they'll receive a WhatsApp link to join. Their onboarding takes about 90 seconds. They can be on the standard jarr; partner features work regardless of plan on either side.

Are more currencies coming?

Yes. AED, GBP, USD, INR, and EUR are supported at launch. Additional currencies are on the roadmap — message us on WhatsApp if there's one you need urgently.

How are exchange rates calculated?

Rates come from ExchangeRate-API. They're refreshed every time you check your status or goals list. There's no markup applied — you see the mid-market rate.
