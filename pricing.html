Questions

Frequently asked.

Do I need a credit card to start?

No. thejarr requires nothing but your WhatsApp number. You only enter payment details if you choose to upgrade to thejarr+.

What happens if I hit 3 goals on thejarr?

thejarr will let you know and offer two options: upgrade to thejarr+ for unlimited goals, or archive an existing goal to free up a slot. Your history is always preserved - archiving just removes a goal from your active list.

What's the difference between basic insights and the myjarr dashboard?

All users get basic in-chat insights: check your balance with status, list all goals with goals, and view goal history with history. thejarr+ unlocks the full myjarr dashboard — a live web page with burn-down charts, currency breakdowns, contribution leaderboards, and partner stats — plus summary on demand and a weekly push summary straight to your WhatsApp. Send myjarr to get your secure dashboard link any time.

Can I cancel thejarr+ at any time?

Yes. Send "cancel subscription" in WhatsApp and thejarr will guide you through it. You keep thejarr+ access until the end of the billing period. After that, your account reverts to thejarr and your 3 most recent goals stay active.

Is my partner's WhatsApp number stored?

We store your WhatsApp number (required to receive messages) and your display name. We never store chat message content beyond what's needed to process your commands. Full details in our privacy policy.

Does my partner need a thejarr account?

Yes - when you invite a partner, they'll receive a WhatsApp link to join. Their onboarding takes about 90 seconds. They can be on thejarr; partner features work regardless of plan on either side.

Are more currencies coming?

Yes. AED, GBP, USD, INR, and EUR are supported at launch. Additional currencies are on the roadmap - message us on WhatsApp if there's one you need urgently.

How are exchange rates calculated?

Rates come from ExchangeRate-API. They're refreshed every time you check your status or goals list. There's no markup applied - you see the mid-market rate.
