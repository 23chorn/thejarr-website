Data Deletion Request

You have the right to request deletion of your personal data held by thejarr. We will process all requests within 30 days.

What data we hold

When you use thejarr, we store:

  • Your WhatsApp phone number
  • Your chosen display name
  • Your savings goals, target amounts, deadlines, and deposit history
  • Your subscription status (if applicable)
  • Interaction logs (command types, timestamps) for service reliability

We do not store the raw text content of your WhatsApp messages beyond what is needed to process a command. Full details are in our Privacy Policy.

How to request deletion

Choose either method below. Both are monitored and will receive a confirmation response.

💬
Via WhatsApp

Open your existing chat with thejarr and send the command below. We will confirm deletion within 30 days.

delete my account
✉️
Via Email

Email us from the address associated with your account, or include your WhatsApp number in the request.

Email us

What happens next: Once your request is received, we will delete or anonymise all personal data associated with your account within 30 days. You will receive a confirmation via WhatsApp or email. Billing records may be retained for the period required by applicable financial regulations.

Questions

If you have questions about your data or this process, contact us at privacy@thejarr.co.
