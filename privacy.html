Privacy Policy

Effective date: 1 March 2026  ·  Last updated: 1 March 2026

thejarr ("we", "our", or "us") operates a WhatsApp-based savings tracking service accessible at thejarr.co. This Privacy Policy explains what personal information we collect, how we use it, who we share it with, and your rights regarding that information.

By using thejarr - including messaging our WhatsApp Business number - you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the service.

1. Who we are

thejarr is a personal finance tool that operates via WhatsApp. Users send text commands to our WhatsApp Business number to create savings goals, log deposits, and track progress. We are based in Dubai, United Arab Emirates.

For privacy enquiries, contact us at: chris@thejarr.co

2. Information we collect

2.1 Information you provide directly

  • WhatsApp phone number - provided automatically when you message our WhatsApp Business number. This is required to receive and send messages.
  • Display name - the name you choose during onboarding (e.g., "Alex"). This is used to personalise your experience.
  • Savings goal data - goal names, target amounts, currencies, deadlines, and deposit amounts you send via WhatsApp commands.
  • Partner phone number - only if you voluntarily share a shared savings goal with another user. Their number is provided by you and used solely to link your goals.

2.2 Information collected automatically

  • Message timestamps - the time commands are sent, used for deposit tracking and the 24-hour undo window.
  • Interaction logs - a record of command types sent (e.g., "deposit logged", "goal created") for service reliability and abuse prevention. We do not store the raw text of your messages beyond what is needed to process a command.

2.3 Payment information (thejarr+ plan subscribers)

If you subscribe to thejarr+, billing is handled entirely by Stripe, Inc. We do not store your card details. Stripe provides us with a customer ID, subscription status, and billing period - nothing more.

2.4 Dashboard access (thejarr+)

When you request a thejarr+ dashboard link, we generate a short-lived, single-use secure token. We log that the link was created and when it was last accessed. No additional personal data is collected during dashboard use.

3. How we use your information

  • To provide the savings tracking service - processing your WhatsApp commands and sending responses.
  • To calculate savings progress, projections, and currency conversions.
  • To send milestone celebrations, reminders, and status updates you request.
  • To link shared goals between you and a partner user.
  • To manage your subscription and billing via Stripe.
  • To prevent abuse and ensure service integrity.
  • To respond to support requests sent to our email or WhatsApp.
  • To comply with applicable legal obligations.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or sale to third parties.

4. Who we share your information with

4.1 Meta Platforms (WhatsApp)

Our service operates through the WhatsApp Business API, provided by Meta Platforms, Inc. All messages you send to our WhatsApp Business number are transmitted via Meta's infrastructure and are subject to Meta's own Privacy Policy and Terms of Service. WhatsApp messages are end-to-end encrypted in transit.

4.2 Stripe

Payment processing for thejarr+ subscriptions is handled by Stripe, Inc. We share only what is necessary to create and manage your subscription.

4.3 ExchangeRate-API

Currency conversion rates are fetched from ExchangeRate-API. No personal data is sent to this service - only the currency codes required to retrieve rates.

4.4 Infrastructure providers

Our backend services are hosted on cloud infrastructure. Providers may have access to data as part of their standard hosting services and are bound by data processing agreements.

4.5 Legal requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or a government authority, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of thejarr, our users, or others.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to any third party for commercial purposes.

5. Data retention

  • Active account data - retained for as long as your account is active.
  • Archived/completed goals - retained indefinitely so your history is always preserved, unless you request deletion.
  • After account deletion - we delete or anonymise your data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.
  • Billing records - retained for the period required by applicable financial regulations (typically 7 years).

6. Your rights

Depending on where you are located, you may have rights including:

  • Access - request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction - ask us to correct inaccurate data.
  • Deletion - request that we delete your account and associated data.
  • Restriction - ask us to limit how we use your data.
  • Portability - receive your data in a portable format.
  • Objection - object to certain types of processing.

To exercise any of these rights, message us on WhatsApp with "delete my account" or email chris@thejarr.co. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Data security

We use industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS), access controls, and secure token generation for dashboard links. WhatsApp messages are end-to-end encrypted between you and Meta's servers.

No system is completely secure. If you believe your account has been compromised, contact us immediately at chris@thejarr.co.

8. Children's privacy

thejarr is not intended for users under the age of 13 (or the minimum age required to use WhatsApp in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

9. International data transfers

Your data may be processed in countries outside the UAE, including the United States, where our infrastructure or third-party providers are based. When transferring data internationally, we rely on appropriate safeguards such as standard contractual clauses.

10. Opt-out and account deletion

You can stop using thejarr at any time. To delete your account and all associated data, send "delete my account" on WhatsApp or email chris@thejarr.co. Deletion is processed within 30 days.

To cancel a thejarr+ subscription, send "cancel subscription" on WhatsApp. Access continues until the end of the current billing period.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. For significant changes, we will notify you via WhatsApp. Continued use of the service after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
